The wordlist file `eff_large_wordlist.txt` is bundled here verbatim from
the EFF's published Diceware wordlist (7,776 words, ~12.9 bits of
entropy per word). Each line has the format `<5-digit-dice-roll>\t<word>`.

We use this list to generate human-memorable passphrases for sealed-store
unlock. With 6 words, ≥77 bits of entropy — enough to make scrypt brute
force infeasible.
